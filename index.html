How to make Beans on Toast

  1. Open can of beans, cook in a pan or microwave
  2. Toast 1 or 2 slices of bread
  3. Add beans on top of now toasted bread

Optional:

  • add grated cheese

How to make Cheese on Toast

  1. Grate cheese
  2. Toast one side of the bread slice/slices
  3. Add cheese to untoasted side
  4. Wait until bubbly

How to make Toast

  • Pick a 1 or 2 slices of nice bread
  • Place in toaster
  • Adjust desired time setting
  • Wait
  • Remove toast from toaster

Optional:

  • add grated cheese
